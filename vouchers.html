Voucher

Do you want to give a gift to your friends/family? Buy a voucher for entry to the Heart of Osiris!

What do you need to do?

  • Email us at escapekrakow.kontakt@gmail.com, providing:
    1. Your first and last name
    2. The first and last name or nickname of the recipient
  • In one day, you will receive an email from us with a link for online payment.
  • Pay for the voucher using the link you received.
  • Within a few days, you will receive an electronic voucher with an entry code from us.

What does the recipient need to do?

  • Make a reservation for a visit to ER through the escapekrakow.pl website within 12 months from the purchase of the voucher
  • Select "Voucher" as the payment method
  • In the notes, provide the entry code copied from the voucher
  • Appear at ER at the designated time with the group (max. 5 people including the recipient) and have a great time!

The price of the voucher is 401 PLN, regardless of the number of people in the group who will use it (max. 5 people). Redemption period: 12 months from the date of purchase.
