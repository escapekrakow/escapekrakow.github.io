    Gift Voucher

    1What do you need to do?

    Send a request

    Write to escapekrakow.kontakt@gmail.com providing:

    • Your name and surname
    • Name and surname or nickname of the recipient

    Pay for the voucher

    Within 1 day you will receive a link for online payment. Pay comfortably online.

    Receive the voucher

    Within a few days you will receive an elegant electronic voucher with a unique entry code.

    Visits our website and chooses a convenient date in the booking calendar.

    Selects "Voucher" as the payment method and enters the code from the gift in the comments.

    Have fun with the team

    Shows up at the place with a group (max 5 people) and experiences an amazing adventure!
