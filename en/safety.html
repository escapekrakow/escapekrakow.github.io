    Safety

    Concern for player safety has guided us from the beginning of building the rooms. We want you to feel comfortable and focus 100% on the fun. The rooms have undergone a mandatory evacuation drill under the supervision of a fire safety specialist.

    Fire Safety Standards

    We meet strict fire safety standards. The rooms are built of non-flammable or flame-retardant materials (class B-s2 or better).

    Fire Extinguishers

    Each room is equipped with easily accessible fire extinguishers.

    Emergency Lighting

    Independent emergency lighting is located in every room.

    Safety System

    Safety buttons immediately turn off the game power and turn on full emergency lighting.

    Open Doors

    Room doors are never locked during the game. Exit is always possible.

    Freedom of Movement

    Players are never immobilized or chained during the game.

    Monitoring

    All rooms are constantly monitored. The Game Master watches over the game and is in constant contact.

    Safe Power

    Players do not have direct access to 230V installations. No gas installation or open fire.

    First Aid

    A fully equipped first aid kit is always available on the premises.
