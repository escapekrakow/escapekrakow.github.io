    The game

    Please arrive at the venue 10 minutes before the game starts. Please do not arrive earlier. In the event of being late, we may be forced to shorten your game or even cancel it - unfortunately, no refund is due in such a situation.

    One room is available for players: Heart of Osiris.

    Restrictions

    • People under 13 years of age may take part in the game only in the company of an adult.
    • Participation of people with disabilities or reduced physical fitness - the game only with an accompanying person. Such a situation must be reported in advance. The staff may decide that supervision is required during such a game, or that the game cannot take place for the safety of the participants.
    • The Heart of Osiris room requires good physical fitness from participants, needed to move in a low space (below 1 metre). If in doubt, please contact us.
    • From 2 to 5 players can take part in the game.
    • The game in the Heart of Osiris room lasts 90 minutes. An additional 30 minutes should be reserved for organisational matters.
    • People under the influence of alcohol or other intoxicants will not be allowed to play.
    • Due to the nature of the experience (photos of the room are not published anywhere, so that players can enjoy the surprise), taking photos or recording videos is not allowed.
    • Please leave phones and other devices in the lockers or with the staff.
    • If photos or videos are taken during the game, the game master ends the game and the participants must leave the room.

    Safety rules

    • The main door to the room is always open.
    • Each room has a safety button which opens all other doors, at the same time turning on the emergency lighting, cutting off the power to the room and thereby ending the game.
