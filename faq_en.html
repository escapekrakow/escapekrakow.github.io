What is an Escape Room?

An Escape Room is a live-action game where you have a set amount of time to solve a series of puzzles. The game is complemented by set design, visual effects, and sound effects.

Will I be locked in the room?

No, we never lock players in the rooms. The exit doors always remain open during the game.

How long does the game last?

The duration of the game varies depending on the scenario, usually ranging from 60 to 90 minutes. Of course, you can complete the room much faster if you are skilled enough to solve the puzzles within the designated time.

Can I visit the same room more than once?

The puzzles do not change, but it's worth bringing your friends who haven't been before and experiencing the adventure again, this time as a guide. You're welcome!

Will there be other people with me in the room?

There will only be the group you come with in the room. Our game rooms can accommodate 2 to 5 people. The rooms are designed in a way that they cannot be completed alone.

What do I need to bring with me?

Absolutely nothing! Everything you need will be provided on-site.

What is the age limit?

We do not have an age limit, but individuals under the age of 15 can visit the rooms only under the supervision of an adult.

Can I pay by credit card?

Yes, both card payment and cash payment are possible.

Is playing during the COVID pandemic safe?

During the game, you are in the room only with the people you came with. There is a break between successive groups. The only person(s) you will encounter outside your group is the game master.

I'm going by car, is there parking available?

The venue is located in a neighborhood where there are plenty of available parking spaces nearby. All parking spaces are free of charge.

Can we enter as a group of 6 people?

Yes, it is possible to play with a group of 6 people. Please make a reservation for 5 people and leave a comment stating that there will be more of you. However, please keep in mind that the room is designed for groups of 2-5 people, so some areas may be tight for a larger group.

Can I gift my friends/family entry to the room (voucher)?

Certainly! You can find more information here: Vouchers.
