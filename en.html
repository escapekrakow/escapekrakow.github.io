    OSIRIS' HEART

    Since the cruel Set killed his brother Osiris, Egypt has been plunged into darkness. You have 90 minutes to find the heart of Osiris and restore peace.

    Mission: Retrieve the Heart

    Since the cruel Set killed his brother Osiris, Egypt plunges into a harvest failure. Hunger and darkness prevail. There is only one way to restore happiness and fertility on the banks of the Nile - one must find and release the heart of Osiris. Discover the secrets of ancient Egyptian gods and restore peace to Egypt.

    See the unique scenography prepared especially for the project by a team of artists. The sound system, consisting of 14 high-quality speakers and surround sound, will allow you to move to another world. The room is packed with electronics, and the whole thing is based on the proprietary ER-Engine.

    90 Minutes
    2-5 Players (Optimally 4)
    Level: Advanced
    Surround Sound
